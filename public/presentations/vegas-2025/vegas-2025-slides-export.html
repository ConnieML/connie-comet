The Solution

From Initial Contact to A Promise Fulfilled

Connie transforms fragmented, manual processes into an efficient, trackable, and data-driven workflow.

1
Inbound / Outbound Flow
A client or partner request arrives from any channel. Connie collects, prioritizes, and routes the request into a task queue.
2
Task Creation
Requests are packaged into tasks, sorted by type, and routed to the correct agent or department.
3
Task Processing
Tasks are displayed to available staff based on skill and availability. An agent accepts the task.
4
Administration
Managers have tools to administer workflow, manage staff schedules, monitor performance in real-time.
