From Initial Contact to A Promise Fulfilled

Connie transforms fragmented, manual processes into an efficient, trackable workflow.

1
Inbound / Outbound Flow
Request arrives from any channel. Connie collects, prioritizes, routes to queue.
2
Task Creation
Requests packaged into tasks, sorted, routed to correct agent/department.
3
Task Processing
Tasks displayed to staff based on skill/availability. Agent accepts task.
4
Administration
Managers administer workflow, schedules, monitor performance, report KPIs.
